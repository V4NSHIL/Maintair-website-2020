Welcome to Maintair Aviation Services

What we do

Maintair Aviation provides best in class handling services for regional, national and international airlines. From Pilatus PC12's to Antonov AN124's, Maintair will handle any aircraft that comes our way! With over 30 years of experience providing safe, efficient, cost effective services, Maintair prides itself on building partnerships with our customers and fostering a healthy, safe workplace, which allows our team to grow within the industry. Led by experienced, hands-on Owners, Maintair Aviation has built a reputation of being a Leader in the Handling Industry in Ontario.
